## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**  
A: Yes. No credit card, no trial. The audit is free forever.

**Q: Do you sell my data?**  
A: No. Your tool list and spend data are used only to generate your audit. We do not sell your data to third parties.

**Q: How is this different from just reading the pricing pages myself?**  
A: We cross-reference 8 tools simultaneously, detect redundancies across tools (like paying for both Cursor and Copilot), and flag plan mismatches for your team size—things that aren't obvious until you see them together in one view.

**Q: What is Credex?**  
A: Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits sourced from companies that overforecast usage. We surface these credit opportunities only when your audit shows significant savings potential that could be better spent on your production infrastructure.

**Q: Is the savings estimate accurate?**  
A: It's based on public pricing data verified weekly. The recommendations are conservative—we only flag real downgrade paths or obvious redundancies, not theoretical ones.
