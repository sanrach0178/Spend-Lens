# Go-to-Market Strategy (GTM)

## Target Audience
**Engineering Managers, Founders, and Ops Leads** at seed to Series A startups (5-50 people). These individuals are directly responsible for the "SaaS bloat" in their tool budget. They feel the monthly burn but haven't had the time or data to justify a manual audit.

## Distribution Channels

### 1. High-Signal Communities (Organic)
We will launch with a "Build-in-Public" approach on **r/startups, r/SaaS, and r/ExperiencedDevs**. 
- **The Hook**: "I built a tool that found $340/mo savings in 60 seconds."
- **Strategy**: Share real data (anonymized) on the most common overspending patterns (e.g., Cursor + Copilot redundancy). This positions SpendLens as a value-add utility rather than a self-promotional product.

### 2. Contextual Conversations (Hacker News)
Monitor **"Ask HN"** threads regarding AI tools, SaaS spend, or startup stacks. 
- **Strategy**: When users discuss their stacks, provide a link to SpendLens as a comparison tool. "We see this a lot in our audit data—you're likely overpaying for seats on Claude Max."

### 3. Public Stack Targeting (X/Twitter)
Target founders who share their tech stacks via **StackShare, GitHub Sponsors, or public "How we build" blog posts**.
- **Strategy**: Cold DM with a specific insight: "Hey, saw you're using both Cursor and Copilot—we're seeing teams save ~$20/user/mo by consolidating those. Use this 60-second audit to see the full breakdown."

### 4. Community Partnerships
Engage with the **Cursor and Copilot Discord/Reddit communities**.
- **Strategy**: These users are power users who care about efficiency. Share the "Redundancy Detection" feature as a way to optimize their personal or team workflows.

### 5. The Unfair Advantage (Credex Integration)
Credex already has an existing client list of startups using AI infrastructure credits. 
- **Strategy**: Every existing Credex client will receive the Audit Tool as a free value-add. This creates a feedback loop: the audit saves them money on subscriptions, which they then redirect into Credex-sourced credits for their production infrastructure.

## Week 1 Traction Targets
- **200 Audits Completed**: Validates the core "Time to Value" (TTV) of the product.
- **40 Emails Captured**: A 20% conversion rate to the newsletter/report list.
- **5 Credex Consultation Requests**: High-intent leads for the parent company's core business.
