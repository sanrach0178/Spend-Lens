# Unit Economics & Projections

## The Opportunity Gap
Based on initial market research of Seed/Series A startups:
- **Average Monthly AI Tool Spend**: $800 (spread across Cursor, ChatGPT, Claude, and API tokens).
- **Average Potential Savings**: $340 (42% of total spend).
- **Redirection Potential**: Of that $340 saved, $100-$200 is typically "waste" that can be redirected into production-grade infrastructure (Credex credits).

## Revenue & LTV
- **Credex Business Model**: Reselling discounted AI infrastructure credits.
- **Assumed Margin**: 15–25% on credits sold.
- **Client Value**: A converted Credex client spending $1,000/mo on credits generates ~$200/mo in margin.
- **LTV Calculation**: $200/mo recurring × 24 months = **$4,800 LTV**.

## Conversion Funnel
A conservative model for the SpendLens acquisition engine:
1. **100 Audits**: Top of funnel traffic.
2. **30 Emails**: 30% conversion to the full report (Lead Capture).
3. **5 Consults**: 16% of leads request a Credex consultation.
4. **2 Clients**: 40% close rate on high-intent consultations.

**Conversion Efficiency**: 2% (Audit-to-Client).

## Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- **Organic CAC**: ~$0 (Leveraging community, content, and the viral nature of the results page).
- **Outbound/BDR CAC**: ~$50 per qualified lead (based on engineering time spent on targeted DMs).
- **LTV/CAC Ratio**: $4,800 / $50 = **96x** (extremely high efficiency for a utility-led growth model).

## Path to $1M ARR
To reach $1M in Annual Recurring Revenue for the core Credex credit business via this tool:
- **Target**: 417 clients at $200/mo avg margin (or 208 clients at $400/mo).
- **Required Volume**: 
  - At a 2% audit-to-client conversion rate, we need **20,850 completed audits**.
  - With a current viral coefficient of **0.3x** (each audit result page leads to 0.3 new audits via sharing), this volume is achievable within 12 months of consistent distribution.
